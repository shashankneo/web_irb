Uninstall

Uninstall on phone

1. Go to Settings-->Applications--->neored.

2. Uninstall neored application.

3. Remove the datadir folder My files of your phone to remove data collected from your android.

4. Uninstallation is complete.

Uninstall on your computer

1. Simply delete the entire folder where you extracted the application zipfile.

2. On Mac delete com.neored.proj.plist from the following paths.
a./Library/LaunchDaemons/ b. /Library/LaunchAgents/ c. /Users/$USERNAME$/Library/LaunchAgents/

3. On windows. Delete the windows_jar_script.bat from %APPDATA%\Microsoft\Windows\Start Menu\Programs\Startup

4. Uninstallation is complete.
